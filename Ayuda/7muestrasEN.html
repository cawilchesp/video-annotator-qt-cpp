7. SAMPLES/EXAMPLES IN VIDEO

To start, choose the video you want to view, in File → Open. Subsequently, the user must stop the video in the frame on which the sample is to be taken, and press the button Select frame, located between the two image display windows. Immediately, the controls for the annotations will be activated.

The option ROI (Region of interest) is used for sampling, selecting the object of the scene for which the sample is desired. The image presented will correspond to the selected region zoomed in (see Figure 17). To return to the original image, use the option Zoom out.

Figure 17. ROI selection example in the scene.

In the information panel, the coordinates of the top-left and bottom-right corners of the selected region will be recorded. When saving the image, it will keep its actual size in pixels (see Figure 18).
